Digital Damage Assessment Platform for Stichting RE-CAMP

We developed a fully digital damage assessment certification platform that revolutionized the process for camper and caravan repairers. This innovative solution allows damage experts to efficiently certify damage calculations, streamlining the entire assessment process.

Key Features:

  • Secure user authentication and role-based access control
  • Intuitive damage calculation and reporting tools
  • Real-time collaboration between experts and repairers
  • Automated certification workflow
  • Comprehensive analytics and reporting dashboard
